Merrell , 40 anos - Parq Magazine

Production and Styling

This fashion editorial is a collaboration with Parq Magazine and a team of ETIC students, where I was responsible for the styling, production, and overall creative direction of the shoot.

This project was a tribute to Merrell’s 40th anniversary, celebrating its legacy of innovation, performance, and adventure.

Inspired by the motto 'Não importa quem somos, de onde vimos, do que gostamos ou de como nos movemos,' we worked with the new Merrell 1TRL collection—designed for a trendy, urban audience—while capturing the spirit of exploration and individuality.

From concept to execution, the experience was an incredible opportunity to bring storytelling and fashion together in a dynamic and professional setting.
