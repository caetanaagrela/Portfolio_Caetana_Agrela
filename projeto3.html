3D Sheraton

3D Modeling

- Blender

This render is a true-to-life representation of the conference room in the Sheraton Hotel.

I created a detailed 3D render of a conference room at the Sheraton Hotel using Blender, focusing on realism and atmosphere.

The space is arranged with 13 conference tables in a "U" shape, 39 chairs and 9 pulpits all carefully modeled to reflect the real and professional ambiance of the hotel.

The lighting, wood texture, fabric and metal all are completly identic to the real room.
