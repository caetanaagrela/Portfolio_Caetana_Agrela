Image Restoration

Graphic Imaging for Digital Media

- Adobe Photoshop

The goal of this Image Restoration project was to restore an old, damaged photograph to its former glory.

Through careful application of digital techniques, I brought vintage images back to life, repairing cracks, fading, and other imperfections.

Utilizing a range of tools such as the clone stamp tool, spot healing tool, healing brush, and paint in Adobe Photoshop , the damaged photo was meticulously repaired.

It presented a challenge due to the damage, often requiring the use of multiple tools in the same area.

I dedicated to preserving the authenticity of historical photos while ensuring they regain their original clarity and vibrancy.Also the art of photo restoration extends to a nuanced understanding of different eras and photographic styles.
