SANTOS DA SAUDADE

Brand Identity

- Adobe Photoshop

- Adobe Illustrator

- Figma

Santos da Saudade is a vibrant and youthful bar in Lisbon with a brand identity that I had the pleasure of creating.

From the bold logo to the lively color palette of red and green and eye-catching flyers, every design choice reflects the bar’s fun and energetic vibe.

The branding blends a nostalgic charm of old Portugal with a modern twist, making it the perfect spot for people looking to enjoy great drinks, good music, and unforgettable nights.
